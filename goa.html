Location covered:

Fort Aguada

Location: Sinquerim Beach, Goa, India.
Status: Monument of National Importance
History: Built in 1612 to guard against the Dutch and to provide a freshwater supply to ships

Baga Beach

Location: One of the most popular beaches in North Goa
Atmosphere: Lively and crowded, especially at night when it becomes a party hub
Activities: Offers water sports like parasailing, jet-skiing, boat rides, and banana boat rides
Food and drink: Many shacks and restaurants nearby, offering a range of cuisine

Basilica of Bom Jesus

Location: The church is located in Old Goa, the former capital of Portuguese India
Name: The name "Bom Jesus" is Portuguese for "Good/Infant Jesus."
Architectural style: The basilica is one of the best examples of Baroque architecture and Portuguese colonial architecture in India.

Madeva temple

The temple consists of garbhagriha, antarala and a pillared Nandi mandapa built of basalt.
The four pillars, embellished with intricate carvings of elephants and chains support a stone ceiling decorated with finely carved Ashtoken lotus flowers.

Varca Beach:

Location: Varca village, South Goa, India
Known for: Pristine white sand, crystal-clear waters, and picturesque views
Activities: Swimming, sunbathing, dolphin-watching, and water sports
Facilities: Beach shacks, restaurants, and resorts
Distance from Goa Airport: Approximately 45 kilometers (28 miles)
Popular with: Tourists seeking a calm and tranquil beach experience
